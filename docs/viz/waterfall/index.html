Waterfall Chart

Description

Waterfall charts show the positive or negative accumulation of metrics in a sequence on the axis that can be based on time or category. The end result often looks like a waterfall, starting at the top with a total and stepping down to the bottom.


Minimum Requirements
1 dimension
1 or more measures
